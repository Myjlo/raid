Aggro
Matthias will target a single player at the start of the encounter and use that as target for Oppressive Gaze and Blood Shards.

Corruption
Every 30 seconds past 9:55.
Matthias targets a random player with an orange AoE that damages other players in it.
Corruption can be cleansed in one of the fountains in the Arena, after which it turns from clear to red and cannot be used to cleanse another Corruption for 90 seconds.
Sacrifice
Every 45 seconds past 9:45 up until the Abomination Phase.
Matthias targets a random player to be sacrificed. After 4 seconds that player gets a breakbar that must be broken within 8 seconds.
Blood Shield/Shards
Every 40 seconds.
Matthias shields himself and becomes invulnerable.
Multiple Blood Shards will be launched at the fixated player, which must be reflected to remove 20 stacks of the Blood Shield.
Shards of Rage
Matthias jumps and slams his staff into the ground, launching multiple projectiles around him.
Avoid reflecting these projectiles, rather absorb or evade them.
