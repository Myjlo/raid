Aggro
Slothasor will aggro on the player affected by Fixation.
See mechanics below for more detail.

Fixation
Every 30 seconds.
A random player gets Fixated and functions as "tank".
Fixation will also change when that player either downs or eats an Imbued Mushroom.
Imbued Mushrooms
Every 50 seconds.
A Blue Mushroom spawns at specific locations.
Interact to transform into a Slubling.
Eat a path using Skill #1 to the next Imbued Mushroom (4 or 5 Mushrooms).
Use Skill #5 to transform back to normal.
As a Slubling you are hostile and can be killed by other players!
Tantrum
A series of Orange AoE's appear under each player.
Deals heavy damage and applies
Keep movement to a minimum, once you see 3 AoE's underneath you, dodge.
Volatile Poison
Every 25 seconds starting at 6:35.
A random player becomes affected with Volatile Poison, which when dropped will create an expanding heavily damaging AoE.
Volatile Poison can be dropped with the Special Action Key or automatically after 8 seconds.
Volatile Poison drops when you go down or eat an Imbued Mushroom!
Bind the 'Special Action Key' to something easy to use!
Halitosis
Roughly every 15 seconds.
Slothasor breathes fire forwards, dealing heavy damage and applying
Stand behind Slothasor to avoid.
Slublings
Every 30 seconds.
Slublings spawn and will attack players.
Slublings
Spawns starting from 100% health.
Uses projectiles that corrupt boons.
Applies
Evolved Slublings
Spawns starting from 30% health.
Similar to their regular counterparts, except:
  • Higher amount of health.
  • Projectiles can not be reflected or destroyed.
Hibernation
Every 20% and at 10% health.
Slothasor falls asleep, becomes immune to damage and gains a breakbar.
Break the bar to continue.
Applies when broken.
Spore Release
Every 45 seconds starting from 50% health.
Slothasor starts shaking and releases spores that deal heavy damage and apply
Double-dodge in Slothasors hitbox to avoid most if not all spores.
You can use invulnerability and jump inside Slothasors hitbox to absorb most if not all spores.

Prisoner Cage
The primary objective of this encounter.
Kill enemies and prevent them from damaging the cage.
Results in a wipe if the Cage reaches 0% health.
Saboteurs
Every 30 secondss starting from 6:55.
Wait until the Saboteur reaches the cage and has a breakbar.
Return to the fight after breaking the breakbar.
Mortars
Spawns at 5:25, 4:25, 3:20 and 2:20 Bandit Bombardiers spawn to set up mortars.
Use Sapper Bombs to launch yourself and break their breakbar.
Return to the fight after breaking the breakbar.
The first 3 waves of Bombardiers spawn outside the Camp, the 4th spawns on the upper platforms in the Camp.
Champions
Berg
Spawns at 6:55.
Throw a beehive to increase damage on Berg (effect doesn't stack).
Break his breakbar for easier skill rotations.
Zane
Spawns at 4:55.
Unleash Caged Wargs to increase damage on Zane.
Stand between Zane and the wall so he doesn't damage the Cage.
Narella
Spawns at 2:55.
Throw and ignite Oil Kegs to increase DPS on Narella.
Avoid Fiery Tornado's and Flak Shot.
Environmental Weapons
Beehives
Hanging on Branches throughout the Camp.
Damage them so they drop and stack them at the east gate.
Deals damage and applies
Can also be used to slowly CC Bandit Saboteurs while fighting Narella.
Caged Wargs
6 Wargs are located in the southern part of the Camp, 2 at the northen part.
Interact to release the Wargs.
Can also be used while fighting Narella.
Oil Kegs
5 Oil Kegs are scattered throughout the Camp.
Stack the Oil Kegs on the ledge near the Northern Gate.
Throw them onto Narella and use to ignite for additional damage.

Aggro
Matthias will target a single player at the start of the encounter and use that as target for Oppressive Gaze and Blood Shards.

Corruption
Every 30 seconds past 9:55.
Matthias targets a random player with an orange AoE that damages other players in it.
Corruption can be cleansed in one of the fountains in the Arena, after which it turns from clear to red and cannot be used to cleanse another Corruption for 90 seconds.
Sacrifice
Every 45 seconds past 9:45 up until the Abomination Phase.
Matthias targets a random player to be sacrificed. After 4 seconds that player gets a breakbar that must be broken within 8 seconds.
Blood Shield/Shards
Every 40 seconds.
Matthias shields himself and becomes invulnerable.
Multiple Blood Shards will be launched at the fixated player, which must be reflected to remove 20 stacks of the Blood Shield.
Shards of Rage
Matthias jumps and slams his staff into the ground, launching multiple projectiles around him.
Avoid reflecting these projectiles, rather absorb or evade them.
