Aggro
Vale Guardian will aggro on the player with the highest toughness.

Distributed Magic
Green Circles spawn every 15 seconds in a random location in the same sector as the boss. You have about 5 seconds to get 4 players into them to negate heavy damage.
Unstable Magic Spike
Blue Circles spawn every 9 seconds within melee range of the Vale Guardian. They last for about 2 seconds and then they will explode, dealing damage and teleporting anyone caught in them to a random location. Dodges/evades work against them but you have more than enough time to walk out them.
Seekers
Three Red Seeker orbs spawn every 20 seconds in each of the floor segments. Their goal is to move towards the players and deal heavy damage to them.
Slow them with .
Past 33% an additional Seeker spawns in the middle of the arena!
Vale Division
Vale Guardian will become invulnerable and split up into the 3 Mini-Bosses you've fought before.
You only have a few seconds to get into the right colored section to get the attunement. Once you get this attunement buff, you will receive heavy damage if you are inside the red circle of a different colored guardian unless your guardian has been killed.

You should have decided pre-fight which members of your raid team are going to each guardian. For the Red Guardian you will need at least 2 Condi DPS. The Chronotank should aggro the Green Guardian and move him towards the Blue Guardian, which should meanwhile be getting killed by the rest of the squad. Break the breakbars to kill the guardians.
Bullet Storm
During Vale Division, you will see multiple, slow moving, white orbs causing damage. If one of these orbs touches the Blue Guardian, it grants it Blue Pylon Power rendering it invulnerable until it is stripped.
Unstable Pylon
At 66%, one floor segment will light up and deal heavy damage to players standing in them.
This segment rotates every 20 seconds. The tank should start the boss at the red segment and then drag him clockwise to the next segment as soon as possible to prevent the green circle from spawning too far behind.
Past 33% an additional segment will light up!
Magic Storm
Past 66%, every 30 seconds Vale Guardian will stop moving and gain a breakbar. While this breakbar is active, the boss will constantly spam AoE orbs at the raid. Break this breakbar as soon as it appears or a green circle can spawn too far behind.

Aggro
Gorseval will aggro on the player with the highest toughness.

Spectral Impact
Gorseval will raise his arm up in the air and slam into the ground, knocking people down. This should be evaded or blocked with .
Ghastly Rampage
Gorseval slam the ground repeatedly, creating patterns of black, ghastly material on the floor.
After 3 seconds, the black material deals heavy damage while applying .
Breaking Gorseval's breakbar interrupts this skill.
You can opt to delay the CC if you have low group DPS.
Gorseval gains unstrippable retaliation, don't kill yourself.
World Eater
Gorseval will channel a large AoE that will kill anyone standing in the platform once he finishes channeling the attack.
To counteract this, get him to 66% or 33% before the channel finishes, or kill a Spectral Wall and use the updraft if you don't have high enough group DPS.
Charged Souls
Charged Souls spawn at 66% and 33% in each of the four corners of the room (NE, SE, SW, NW).
They will attempt to reach Gorseval and if they do, this results in a wipe.
Slow them with .
Spectral Darkness
Spectral Darkness are orbs that spawn throughout the room past 66%.
They are harmless initially but they will expand out to an AoE if not killed quickly. Anyone that touches this AoE will get a debuff that reduces their outgoing damage and increases their incoming damage.

To remove this you must get the 2 gold orbs that spawn after you kill an orb. You want to use ranged DPS to clear Spectral Darkness.
Ghastly Prison
Ghastly Prisons appear under every player every 25 seconds past 33%. It is important that you do not get hit by the Ghastly Prison. You have time to dodge out or walk out. If you do end up getting hit by this, you have to use your Special Action Key, or ask your teammates to break your out.
Getting imprisoned is a major DPS loss.

Cannon Duty.

Sabetha is a glorified DPS golem.
Ideally you want 2 mobile DPS classes with a quick burst capability (Weaver) or short ramp up time for condi (Soulbeast/Renegade) on Cannon Duty.

Platform Integrity
In addition to the enrage timer, the platform also has a healthbar.
The platform is slowly damaged as the fight progresses from cannon fire, and can be heavily damaged by Heavy Bombs exploding. If the bar reaches zero, this results in a wipe.
Time Bomb
The closest player to Sabetha will be targeted and explode after 3 seconds.
The explosion will do heavy damage to players and instantly kill any downed players caught in it.
After 50% an additional Time Bomb will spawn!
Flak Shot
Every 7 seconds Sabetha will fire slow moving projectiles at the player furthest away from her.
Once it lands it will put down several AoE's that apply .
Flame Wall
A Flame Wall will spawn roughly every 45 seconds, and when Sabetha returns to the platform.
She will target a random player and rotates around the entire arena counter-clockwise. Any player it touches is instantly killed.
Sapper Bombs
Green bombs are regularly placed on players by Bandit Sappers.
If you have a Sapper Bomb, you have 5 seconds to use it before it times out.
You should throw them at a player on the launchpad in the same direction as the Cannon that is currently firing, so they get launched up to the Cannon and can destroy it.
Bind the 'Special Action Key' to something easy to use!
Cannons
Cannons will spawn around the arena every 30 seconds in order: SWNE SNWE.
You can have at least 2 players assigned to cannons, each taking one in turn.
They will get a debuff that prevents them from being launched for 50 seconds.

Cannons will cover a sector of the platform with AoEs and cause damage to the platforms if not killed quickly.
Heavy Bombs
While you are fighting Kernan, Knuckles or Karde, Heavy Bombs will appear on the platform.
These bombs need to be dealt with otherwise the platform will die.
Simply run up to them and interact to kick them off.
