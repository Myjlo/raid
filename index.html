Disclaimer
The suggested compositions and classes are by no means the absolute best.
They're not compositions for speedclears, and are picked based on what I personally found the most common and reliable compositions, be it with pugs or coordinated groups.

Realistically any composition/class is capable of killing a boss granted mechanics are accounted for.
DPS checks are generally fairly lenient, Find a class you feel comfortable on and remember that it is expected you pull your own weight.
