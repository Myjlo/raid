For Vale Guardian you need at least 2 Condi DPS classes to kill the Red Guardian during the split fase at 66% and 33%. The remaining spots should preferably be filled with Power DPS classes that have a high amount of burst.

Classes below are ordered by Effectiveness/Buffs and Difficulty (for example Engineer brings Pinpoint Distribution but has an incredibly long and complicated rotation).

Mechanics
Mechanics Walkthrough
Distributed Magic
Green Circles spawn every 15 seconds in a random location in the same sector as the boss. You have about 5 seconds to get 4 players into them to negate heavy damage.
Unstable Magic Spike
Blue Circles spawn every 9 seconds within melee range of the Vale Guardian. They last for about 2 seconds and then they will explode, dealing damage and teleporting anyone caught in them to a random location. Dodges/evades work against them but you have more than enough time to walk out them.
Seekers
Three Red Seeker orbs spawn every 20 seconds in each of the floor segments. Their goal is to move towards the players and deal heavy damage to them. They can be immobilized or knocked back.
In Phase 5 an additional Seeker spawns in the middle of the arena!
Vale Division
Vale Guardian will become invulnerable and split up into the 3 Mini-Bosses you've fought before.
You only have a few seconds to get into the right colored section to get the attunement. Once you get this attunement buff, you will receive heavy damage if you are inside the red circle of a different colored guardian unless your guardian has being killed.

You should have decided pre-fight which members of your raid team are going to each guardian. For the Red Guardian you will need at least 2 Condi DPS. The Chronotank should aggro the Green Guardian and move him towards the Blue Guardian, which should meanwhile be getting killed by the rest of the squad. Break the breakbars to kill the guardians.
Bullet Storm
During Phase 2 and 4, you will see multiple, slow moving, white orbs causing damage. If one of these orbs touches the Blue Guardian, it grants it Blue Pylon Power rendering it invulnerable until it is stripped.
Unstable Pylon
Starting from Phase 3, one floor segment will light up and deal heavy damage to players standing in them.
This segment rotates every 20 seconds. The tank should start the boss at the red segment and then drag him clockwise to the next segment as soon as possible to prevent the green circle from spawning too far behind.
In Phase 5 an additional segment is energized!
Magic Storm
Starting from Phase 3, every 30 seconds Vale Guardian will stop moving and gain a breakbar. While this breakbar is active, the boss will constantly spam AoE orbs at the raid. Break this breakbar as soon as it appears or a green circle can spawn too far behind.
Preparation

Assign people to Green Circle Duty and decide who goes where during Phase 2 and Phase 4.
Check your traits/skills/gear, eat your food/utility.

Phase 1
100%

Active Mechanics: Distributed Magic, Unstable Magic Spike, Seekers.

Phase 2 (Vale Division)
66%

Active Mechanics: Blue Guardian - Distributed Magic, Green Guardian - Unstable Magic Spike, Red Guardian - Seekers, Bullet Storm.
Split according to prior decision and kill the Guardians as fast as possible.

Phase 3
66%

Active Mechanics: Distributed Magic, Unstable Magic Spike, Seekers, Unstable Pylon, Magic Storm.
Start in the red segment and keep rotating around every 20 seconds (Tank should move 3 seconds earlier). Call out breakbars and greens. These will sometimes be present at the same time, making it slightly difficult to break for some groups.

Phase 4 (Vale Division)
33%

Active Mechanics: Blue Guardian - Distributed Magic, Green Guardian - Unstable Magic Spike, Red Guardian - Seekers, Bullet Storm.
Split according to prior decision and kill the Guardians as fast as possible.

Phase 5
33%

Active Mechanics: Distributed Magic, Unstable Magic Spike, Seekers, Unstable Pylon, Magic Storm.
An additional floor segment will light up and an additional Seeker will spawn.

Start in the red segment and keep rotating around every 20 seconds (Tank should move 5 seconds earlier).
Call out breakbars and greens. These will sometimes be present at the same time, making it slightly difficult to break for some groups.

Stay calm, stay focused. Things can get hectic, but losing focus will only make it worse.
